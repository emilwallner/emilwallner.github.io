If you’re building a blog, digital self-expression is not only important, it’s a fundamental freedom we are all afforded, and one we should embrace.

In today’s digital landscape, having a space to create and write what you like and how you like is valuable, if even in principle. You make the rules. You needn’t care whether people like, retweet, upvote, applaud, or comment on your words. You can write mindfully—with meaning, substance, and creative freedom.
